Main Features

Weather Forcasting

Provides real-time weather forcasting and early warning systems enabling farmers make proactive decisions for the best crop yield.

Pest and Disease identification

Farmers provide images or description of sick crops and receive AI powered recommendations for treatment and prevention.

Market and Price forcasting

Analyze markets in real-time and provide recommendations on when, where and how much to sell their produce

Personalised recommendations

Uses machine learning techniques to advice farmers on the best agricultaral practices.
